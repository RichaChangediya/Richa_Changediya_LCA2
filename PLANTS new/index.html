Plant Care Tips

Light

Most indoor plants need bright, indirect sunlight to thrive. Place them near windows but avoid direct sun exposure.

Water

Water thoroughly when the top inch of soil feels dry. Ensure proper drainage to prevent root rot.

Temperature

Keep plants in rooms between 65-80°F (18-27°C). Protect them from cold drafts and sudden temperature changes.

Humidity

Many indoor plants thrive in humid environments. Use a humidifier or mist regularly to maintain moisture.
